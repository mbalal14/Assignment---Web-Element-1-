About Us

City Explorer is a digital service aiming to provide travellers and geography enthusiasts with an intuitive way to discover and learn about cities around the world.

Why explore new Cities?

To learn about the different cultures, try different things which you have never tried before, discover new cuisines, maybe even look at the different museums or art galleries. You will return home with a great experience. And it doesn't stop there.

Why is exploring so important?

Exploring is to travel through the unknown to learn or discover new information about the place. Discoveries in geography, medicine, space and more that have changed the world for the better.

Why do people visit different Cities?

There are major events found in different cities which are a big reason to visit. The annual Chinese New Year in Chinatown is a fantastic experience. Different Cities boast large populations, which can sustain major concert tours, fireworks for special occasions, ethinic celebrations and more.
